About EasyCard

EasyCard is a tiny free website that lists loyalty card barcodes for popular supermarkets. Show the barcode at the till and you get the member discount — without filling in another sign-up form, installing another app, or handing over your email address.

Why it exists

Every supermarket has its own loyalty programme. Albert Heijn has the Bonuskaart, Tesco has the Clubcard, Sainsbury's has Nectar, Lidl has Lidl Plus, Carrefour has its carte fidélité, and on it goes. The discounts are real — but signing up for a dozen programmes means a dozen accounts, marketing emails, and apps you'll never use again.

EasyCard collects shareable loyalty card barcodes in one place so you can scan and go. Bookmark this site on your phone, pull it up at the checkout, scan, done.

How EasyCard works

  • Pick a supermarket from the home page.
  • The barcode renders client-side in your browser using JsBarcode.
  • Tap Fullscreen for scan and hold your phone under the scanner.
  • No accounts, no tracking beyond standard privacy-respecting analytics.

A note on barcodes

The numbers on this site are community-shared loyalty codes intended as a fallback for shoppers who don't want to register. If a code stops working at your local store, please open an issue on GitHub and I'll update it. EasyCard does not bypass payment or membership controls — it just saves you a signup form to get a publicly advertised discount.
